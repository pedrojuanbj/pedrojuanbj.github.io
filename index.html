About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Pedro J. Buigues, a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT).

I recently completed my PhD in Computational Biophysics in the Rosta Group (University College London), where I developed machine‑learning‑enhanced sampling methods for molecular dynamics simulations.

I hold a BSc in Biotechnology and an MSc in Advanced Molecular & Therapeutic Biotechnology.

  • Proficient in Python, bash/Linux, and HPC environments
  • Extensive experience with MD engines (NAMD, AMBER, GROMACS, OPENMM)
  • Machine‑learning toolkits (TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit‑learn)
  • Visualization & analysis (PyMOL, VMD, MDAnalysis)
  • NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute – Fundamentals of Deep Learning Certificate

✨ Outside the lab I enjoy musicals, drama, and choir singing.
